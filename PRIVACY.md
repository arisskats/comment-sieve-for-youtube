# Privacy Policy Draft

## Comment Sieve for YouTube

Effective date: `TBD before publication`

Comment Sieve for YouTube is designed to process YouTube comment text locally in your browser. The extension does not require an account, does not transmit comment text to external services, and does not include built-in analytics or advertising trackers.

## Information processed

The extension reads comment text that is already visible on supported YouTube watch pages in order to classify and filter those comments in the page UI.

The extension also stores the following settings locally in your browser:

- Filter preferences
- Greeklish mode preference
- Custom blocked keywords

This information is stored using Chrome extension local storage on your device.

## Information not collected

The extension does not intentionally collect or transmit:

- Personal information
- Account credentials
- Payment information
- Browsing history outside supported YouTube pages
- Comment text to remote servers

## How data is used

Data is used only to:

- Label comments in the current page
- Hide or show comments according to your settings
- Remember your saved preferences on your device

## Data sharing

No data is sold or shared with third parties by the extension itself because the extension is designed to operate locally in the browser.

## Data retention

Saved settings remain in your browser's local extension storage until you change them, remove the extension, or clear extension storage through your browser.

## Security

The extension is designed with a minimal-permission model and local processing approach. However, no software can guarantee absolute security.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version should be published with a new effective date before or alongside the updated extension release.
